Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi

Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi (kaz. Qozha Akhmet Yassaui kesenesi) - the Mausoleum of on the grave of the Turkic poet and founder of the Sufi Order Yassavi Khoja Ahmed Yasawi, located in the city of Turkestan region of Kazakhstan. It is the central object on the territory of the historical and cultural museum-reserve "Khazret-Sultan".

Esentai mall

Esentai mall is a shopping center located in Almaty city, prospectus Al-Farabi 77 Skyscraper Esentai tower is near to it and they were developed by Skidmore, Owings and Merril. Hotel Ritz-Carlton is located in Esentai tower. Esentai mall is luxurious shopping center, featuring premium brands like Gucci, Hugo Boss and etc. Cost of the project Esentai tower is 1.5 billion US dollars.

Skating rink Medeo

Medeo (kaz. Medeu - support) is a high-mountain sports complex located in the high-mountain tract of Medeu at an altitude of 1691 meters above sea level. It's located in Almaty city and was built in 1972, opened in the same year. Medeo can fit 8100 people. Medeo is the largest high-mountain complex in the world with ice-ara of 10500 square meters. It is a state architectural monument of Republican significance.
